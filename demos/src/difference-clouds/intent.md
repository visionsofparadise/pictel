# Difference Clouds

A purely-generated abstract texture — two layers of cloud noise at different scales and seeds, combined by absolute-difference so the result is a marbled field of contrasting bands. Long flowing veins run through tighter regions of high contrast. Colours are pulled toward warm rust and cool teal by a saturation lift. The look is the photoshop "Render Clouds → Difference Clouds" technique distilled, with the marbled-paper / oil-and-water aesthetic that produces.
